Who are we?

AmplifyBiz is a non-profit and student-led agency dedicated to supporting and empowering enterprises in the Bay Area and beyond. With a passion for making a positive difference, we provide an array of free services, including web development, SEO, digital marketing, and data analytics, tailored to the specific needs of small businesses.

Founded in 2022, our commitment lies in helping businesses overcome obstacles and thrive in today's dynamic marketplace. Leveraging the power of technology and data-driven insights, we aim to maximize impact and drive sustainable business development. Having collaborated with numerous individuals and organizations, we take pride in the impact we make within our community.

By designing our very own data science algorithms and conducting extensive economic impact studies, our support aligns precisely with the unique requirements of each business we serve. At AmplifyBiz, we envision a thriving and resilient community of enterprises, capable of flourishing even in the face of challenges. Join us on this journey of growth, collaboration, and success as we amplify businesses and create lasting positive change.

Our Mission

1. Community Focused Solutions

With sophisticated data science algorithms, we identify the best audience, locations, and platforms for marketing, ensuring impactful and engaging campaigns that leave a lasting impression on potential customers. Our dedication to making a positive difference in the community drives us to deliver tailored solutions that help businesses thrive.

2. Supporting Local Ventures

We are dedicated to empowering small businesses, especially those with limited budgets for marketing. Through our tailored strategies and free services, we provide support to help them thrive in a competitive digital landscape. Our mission is to foster growth and success for every business we serve.

3. Nurturing Young Talent

At Amplify Biz, we nurture and support young talents to become agents of positive change in their communities. Through mentorship and active community engagement, we instill the value of using one's skills and knowledge to make a meaningful impact on society. Together, we work towards creating a future where the tech industry becomes a force for positive transformation, and every individual contributes to building a better and more inclusive world.

Our Values

At AmplifyBiz, our values are the driving force behind everything we do. These values shape our actions, decisions, and interactions with our clients and the community. They are the pillars that support our mission and guide us towards making a positive impact on businesses and individuals alike

  • Innovation: We continuously strive to innovate and embrace new technologies to provide cutting-edge solutions to our clients.
  • Empowerment: We empower small businesses to succeed by offering free services and tailored strategies that level the playing field in the digital landscape.
  • Community: Our commitment to the community drives us to give back and support local ventures, nurturing young talent and fostering a culture of positive change.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration and actively engage with our clients to understand their unique needs and challenges.
  • Impact: We measure our success by the impact we create. Our insights showcase that our efforts yield meaningful and lasting results.
