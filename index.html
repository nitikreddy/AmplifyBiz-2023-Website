About us

Partnering for Progress:
Our Dedication to Community Impact

AmplifyBiz is a nonprofit student-led agency dedicated to supporting and empowering enterprises in the Bay Area. With a passion for making a positive difference, we provide an array of free services, including web development, SEO, digital marketing, and data analytics, tailored to the specific needs of small businesses. Leveraging the power of technology and data-driven insights, we aim to maximize impact and drive sustainable business development.
